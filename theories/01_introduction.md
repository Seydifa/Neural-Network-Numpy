# 01 - Introduction: The Quest for Artificial Intelligence

Welcome to the **Neural Network from Scratch** project! This repository is designed to be a bridge between mathematical theory and practical software engineering.

## Why build from scratch?

In the age of TensorFlow and PyTorch, why spend time writing matrix multiplications by hand?
1. **True Understanding**: You don't really know how a car works until you take the engine apart.
2. **Mathematical Intuition**: You'll see exactly how calculus and linear algebra collaborate to "learn".
3. **No Magic**: You'll realize that "AI" isn't magic—it's just well-organized math and optimization.

## The Learning Roadmap

We have organized the theories into a logical sequence to help you grasp the concepts step-by-step:

1. **[Anatomy of a Neuron](02_anatomy_of_a_neuron.md)**: Understanding the building blocks.
2. **[Activation Functions](03_activation_functions.md)**: The "decision-makers" of the network.
3. **[Forward Propagation](04_forward_propagation.md)**: How data flows through the system.
4. **[Loss Functions](05_loss_functions.md)**: Measuring how "wrong" the model is.
5. **[Backpropagation](06_backpropagation.md)**: The heart of learning (the chain rule in action).
6. **[Optimization Algorithms](07_optimization_algorithms.md)**: How the model actually improves over time.

## Prerequisites

To get the most out of this, you should be familiar with:
- **Python & NumPy basics**: Array operations and broadcasting.
- **High-school Calculus**: Specifically, what a derivative represents (a rate of change).
- **Linear Algebra**: Matrix-vector multiplication.

Ready? Let's dive into the **Anatomy of a Neuron**!
